The Beatles Tribute Page

Bio

The Beatles were an English rock band formed in Liverpool in 1960. They are regarded as one of the most influential bands of all time as they were integral to the development of 1960s counterculture and popular music's regonition as an art form.

Rooted in skiffle, beat, and 1950s rock and roll, their sound incorporated elements of classical music and traditional pop in innovative ways. They were particularly heavily inspired by the Beach Boy's album "Animal Sounds". The band later explored music styles ranging from ballads and Indian music to psychedelia and hard rock.
